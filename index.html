About

Welcome to my homepage! I am a 5th year PhD Candidate in the Institute for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at University at Buffalo, The State University of New York, under the supervision of Prof. Ashlee N. Ford Versypt . My PhD research is focus on building Computational Multiscale Modeling of Metastatic Cancer Cell Migration and Invasion. I employ mathematical models, machine learning models and data science techniques in my investigtion and research. I spend the summer of 2022 with JPMorgan Chase & Co. as an Artifical Intelligence and Data Science Summer Associate. The summer of 2021 was spent at Oak Ridge National Laboratory as a Geospatial Data Science Research Intern. I have more than six (6) years of experience working in the academia, US Govt National Laboratory, financial industry and IT.
